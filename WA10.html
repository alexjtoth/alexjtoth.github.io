Steps

When trying to change how much of the codeblock you want animated, there are many ways to do this. When a singular number is split up with | in the code, only that line of code will appear animated. When the line numbers are separated by commas, it means those two lines will be animated. And last, when there is a dash between two line numbers, that means all the lines between those two numbers will appear animated.
