Data Analysis

The 3pt Explosion in the NBA

This was a project completed for my Computational Reasoning 2 Course (INFO 2201). The project description was, tell something interesting from the data. I decided to look at NBA data from mainly the 2018-19 Season and from the previous 20 years. I wanted to find patterns in the data. I found that 3-point shooting has increased dramatically in the last couple years. This project forced me to scrape data of the web and deeply analyze it. I have attached a pdf of my full project where I delved into the 3-point revolution.



Technical Communication

In my Technical Communication Course (WRTG 3035), me and my group of three were tasked with drafting and finalizing a professional parent manual for Forest Time Kindergarten in Perrysburg, OH.

This process took 2 months of trial and error. We intend for this to be an easy to read manual that will greatly help confused parents at Forest Time Kindergarten. This group project helped a small business and improved my technical writing skills.
